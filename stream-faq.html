Stream FAQ

What is this?

This is where you can find the answers to a bunch of questions you might have running through your head while you watch my stream!

What kind of game are you making?

I'm making a 2D Top-Down Action-Adventure game (with some RPG elements mixed in there). I know that's a bit much so let me break it down for you.

  • It'll be a top-down 2D game, not a platformer. This means that the camera is above the player and you can move up, down, left, right, and diagonally.
  • An action-adventure game basically means that all combat will happen in real time. There are no battle scenes or anything like that. When you see an enemy, you attack it and defeat it (or it defeats you!).
  • The RPG elements will probably come in the form of leveling up weapons, magic, and other powerups.

What programming languages / libraries / tools will you be using?

  • Programming language: C++
  • Libraries: SDL2 / OpenGL
  • Tools: CLion IDE, Tiled map editor, Photoshop

What is the theme of this game?

I really like this question and enjoy answering it. Ever since I started making games, I've had a certain idea for a game I want to create and I finally have the opportunity to do so.

There are two main themes in this game:

  • Dreams
  • Time
My goal when creating games is to evoke as much emotion from the player as possible. I believe that a great game is one that you feel for awhile after playing it. Both of these themes, when used properly, have the ability to do exactly this.

Why are you streaming the development of this game on Twitch?

One of the major reasons I am streaming the development of this game on Twitch is for the interaction with all of you. As a game developer, my goal is to get my game into as many hands as possible. I want everyone to have the experience that I am creating. Twitch is a great way to accomplish this.

Another reason is that it gives you a way to interact with me. I can answer any questions you have related to programming, game development, or anything else. I also think it's great to get to know everyone who is interested in my games, so please come say hi!
